Our Mission:

Our mission is to assist people with controling their negative emotions.
Here are some of the tools that you can use:

On the entries page, you can write out your feelings and save your writing or destroy it.

Reveiw your entries and destroy them, if nessesary.

On the affirmation page, a random possitive message will be generated and you can choose to have another.

Finally, we have a mood tracker to help you keep track of your feelings.
